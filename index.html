Sobre

Java and 35+ years of experience are the foundation of everything I build. Today, as a Software Engineer and Tech Lead at Atos in Lisbon, that foundation supports a modern stack: Node.js, React, Vue.js, microservices, Kafka, Grafana, Prometheus, Redis and PostgreSQL. I lead technical teams, run agile ceremonies as a certified Scrum Master, and champion code quality, observability and systems built to scale.

Those 35+ years — from ERP systems and B2B integrations with banks and government agencies to cloud SaaS and mobile — have given me an architectural perspective that is hard to acquire quickly. Having seen patterns fail at scale makes me a more effective tech lead: I spot risks earlier, mentor engineers across all seniority levels and make architectural decisions grounded in real trade-offs, not trends.

Bachelor's in Software Engineering from Martha Falcão Wyden, certified Scrum Master. With Java as my root, I also build with Node.js, React, Vue.js, Python and mobile solutions — and I treat observability (Grafana, Prometheus) and data persistence (PostgreSQL, Redis, SQLite) as first-class citizens in every project.
